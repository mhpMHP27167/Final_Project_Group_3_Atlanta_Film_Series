Submission Guidelines

Formats

Entries should be submitted through FilmFreeway, via an online source.

Selection and Notification

Programming decisions are made by the festival directors and our selection committee. If accepted, we will notify filmmakers by email. After acceptance, the exhibition format will need to be in our office two weeks before the festival. We do not pay rental fees for submitted work that is programmed.

Accolades

Although the art of filmmaking is not competitive, The Atlanta Film Series does give accolades to films selected as best in various categories.

Confirmation of Entry

Feel free to email us at: atlantafilmseries@gmail.com if you would like confirmation of entry.
